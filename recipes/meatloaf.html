Meatloaf

Description

This is a very easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf. It won't take long to make at all, and it's quite good!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons brown suggar
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • ⅓ cup kectchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degree C.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread OR cracker crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased loaf pan, or form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased baking dish.
  3. In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
  4. Bake at 175 degree C for 1 hour.
